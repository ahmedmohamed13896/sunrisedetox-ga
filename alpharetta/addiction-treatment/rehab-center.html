You Can’t Get Into Drug and Alcohol Rehab Without This

If you’re looking for a rehab center for you or a loved one, you won’t be allowed to check in until you’ve detoxified your body of drugs and alcohol. This means recovery starts with a 5- or 10-day detox, depending on substance and length of time of you’ve been using.

Withdrawing on your own can be dangerous and sometimes even fatal.

Depending on the substance, withdrawal symptoms include:

  • Fever, diarrhea, nausea, and vomiting
  • Confusion, delirium, panic attacks, and sometimes hallucinations
  • Seizures and shaking

However, you can gently rid your body of drugs and alcohol with medical detox. The purpose of medical detox is not only to keep you safe, but to also make sure you’re comfortable and relaxed, so you can start mentally preparing for rehab.

After detox, we’ll help you get into a rehab center that’s the best fit for your needs so you can continue your recovery for a lifetime of health and sobriety.
